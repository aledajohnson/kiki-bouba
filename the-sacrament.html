The Sacrament of the Floof

Once you have completed your sacred unlearning, your spirit is ready to receive the sacrament of the Softlands. The sacrament must be fed to you by another. Please find a comrade willing to feed the sacrament to you.

As it dissolves, pause to appreciate the boundlessness of our hearts, the roundness of our brains, and the curves in our path. Life was never meant to be a straight line. Let this beautiful togetherness in floof sustain us through many more cycles.
