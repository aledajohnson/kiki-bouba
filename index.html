Welcome, Beautiful Souls

For too long, you have walked the exhausting path of the Kiki, seduced by the path of logic, the sharp tyranny of reason, and the rigid violence of straight lines. We held space for your jaggedness, but we knew your spirit was crying out for something softer.

The Great Floof is your sanctuary. In this sacred, un-structured space, we are going to gently dissolve your rigidness. The Kiki world wants you to believe that life is a grid to be calculated, scheduled, and engineered. But we know the cosmos is actually a warm, rolling marshmallow of shared soul and endless rhythm. Here, we give you total permission to stop straining against the sharp edges, surrender the timelines, and finally melt into the open, fluid current of the world. Open your hearts, un-bone your spines, and prepare to ooze.
