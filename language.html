The Unlearning

Three gentle practices for dissolving your jaggedness.

1. Cognitive Re-Softening

We invite seekers to step away from the violent act of knowing.

Logic is just the mind building armor out of sharp corners. Through gentle cognitive re-softening, we replace hard facts with soft possibilities.

You don't need to analyze the river.

Just enjoy swimming in it.

Begin your cognitive re-softening in the massage area of the wellness center.

2. Boundary Dissolution

The words "I" and "me" create an unnecessary fence between your ego and the cosmos.

Here, we gently unlearn individuality.

Continue your boundary dissolution in the cuddle puddle.

3. Somatic Sound Cleansing

Hard consonants like T and K create subtle friction in the nervous system.

Our linguistic detox helps release these aggressive phonemes until your voice returns to its natural state: a low-frequency ancestral hum.

During the retreat, please favor soft language.

  • Instead of "I think…", try "The universe is considering…"
  • Instead of "No," try "Not for this version of reality."
