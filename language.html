The Unlearning

Three gentle practices for dissolving your jaggedness.

1. Cognitive Re-Softening

We invite our seekers to step away from the violent act of "knowing." Logic is simply the mind trying to build an armor of sharp corners. Through gentle cognitive re-softening, we learn to exile hard facts, replacing them with "soft possibilities" and "pillowy intuitions." Sometimes we don't need to analyze the river; just simply enjoy swimming in it.

To begin your cognitive resoftening, please visit the massage area.

2. Boundary Dissolution

The words "I" and "Me" are incredibly pointy. They create a sharp fence between your ego and the cosmos. In our advanced integration circles, we gently unlearn individual containment. We replace isolating words with fluid, collective containers. You are no longer an individual navigating a schedule; you are a localized ripple in the communal pool of jelly.

To start your boundary dissolution, visit the cuddle puddle space at our retreat center.

3. Somatic Sound Cleansing

Every time you pronounce a hard, explosive consonant like a T or a K, your nervous system experiences a micro-trauma of friction. Our linguistic detox program helps you unlearn the aggressive syntax of your past. By exiling these jagged, toxic phonemes from your internal narrative, you allow your vocal cords to return to their natural, ancestral state of constant, low-frequency humming.

During the retreat, try using alternative, soft language. Here are some examples.

  • Hubappuby Bubirthdubay Ubash uband Kubelluby
  • Wubee lubovubee oubour frubiendubs
  • Lubovubee ubis Buboububa, lubong lubivubee Buboububa
